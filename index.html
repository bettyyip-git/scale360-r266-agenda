R266 Planning: Key Outcomes

8 Strategic Outcomes

Our R266 planning aims to align on eight critical dimensions that define product direction, customer value, execution readiness, and program health.

1. Scale360 Elevator Pitch

What is Scale360 in one compelling pitch? How do we articulate the value proposition to customers, stakeholders, and internal teams?

Craft a clear, concise elevator pitch that captures Scale360's unique value, target audience, and differentiation — something the entire team can rally behind and communicate consistently.

📍 Related Topics: Day 3 — Elevator Pitch Workshop; Future State User Journey (Day 1)

2. Product Vision & Timeline

What are we building, what will the customer experience be (external customers + internal personas), and when will it be available?

We need a clear future state user journey that the team can rally behind, with concrete timelines and customer touchpoints defined.

📍 Related Topics: Future State User Journey (Day 1), Topic 1.1-1.7

3. ProM & Scale Center Strategy

What's our plan for ProM and Scale Center? How do these components fit into the broader Scale360 roadmap?

Align on migration strategy, feature timelines, EOL dates, and how we transition existing customers to Scale360.

📍 Related Topics: Topic 2.3 — ProM & Scale Center Migration to Scale360 & EOL

4. Dreamforce Engagement

Do we have a Dreamforce presence this cycle? If so, what's the scope and commitment level?

Clarify what demos, presentations, or commitments we're making for Dreamforce (Sep 15, 2026) and what's required to deliver them.

⏱️ Deadline: Dreamforce Sep 15, 2026 — 76 days from today

5. Service Owner Onboarding

What's the onboarding experience for Scale360 service owners? How do we set them up for success from day one?

Define the process, tools, documentation, and support model for teams adopting Scale360 services.

📍 Related Topics: Topic 1.7 — Scale360 Service Ownership Onboarding

6. Key Decisions

What are the must-make decisions to unblock progress? Where do we need executive alignment or cross-functional commitment?

Surface critical decision points across product strategy, architecture, resourcing, and scope that require leadership sign-off.

📍 Related Topics: Day 3 — Outcomes, Key Decisions & Action Items; Decision Log

7. Program Health & Work Streams

How do we track program health across work streams? What are the critical metrics and rhythms to maintain visibility?

Establish tracking mechanisms, KPIs, and regular checkpoints to ensure the program stays on track from planning through GA.

📍 Key Dates: R266 FF (Nov 19), R266 RF (Dec 10), Product GA (Jan 31, 2027)

8. Dependencies & Integration Points

What are the critical dependencies on other teams/programs? Where do we need upstream commitments or cross-team coordination to unblock Scale360 work?

Map dependencies across engineering, product, UX, legal, security, and external teams. Identify integration points that could block progress if not addressed early.

📍 Related Topics: Topic 2.2 — Pricing & CTS; Topic 2.5 — Product Launch to GA; Topic 2.6 — Architecture Comparison

Summary

These eight outcomes balance what (product/roadmap/pitch), who (customers, service owners), when (timeline, Dreamforce), and how (decisions, tracking, dependencies) — providing a complete picture for R266 strategic planning.
